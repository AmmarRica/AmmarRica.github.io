Deck Delver

Memory Match Dungeon

Flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs — but watch out: the deck is full of goblins, weapons, jokers, coins, and potions.

  • 🗡️ Weapon pairs stack in your arsenal
  • 👹 Goblin pairs spend a weapon — or cost HP (the only thing that hurts you)
  • 🧪 Potion pairs restore health
  • 🪙 Coin pairs and playing-card pairs earn gold
  • 🃏 Jokers swap positions with whatever you flip them with
  • Clear the board to descend to the next level
