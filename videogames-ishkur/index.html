🎮 Videogames Ishkur

An interactive guide to how video-game genres spawned, split, and evolved — from the first pixels to today. Inspired by Ishkur's Guide to Electronic Music.

Tap any genre block to learn more  ·  Swipe / scroll right to travel through time  ·  Mouse-wheel or pinch to zoom  ·  ★ starred genres are truly unique — no children, no direct lineage  ·  circles = individual games, sized by cultural impact
