Welcome to my personal site! 🎉

This is the first post on my new Jekyll-powered blog. I built this site to have a place to share my thoughts, document things I'm learning, and work through ideas in public.

## What to expect here

I plan to write about:

- **Software development** — tips, tricks, deep dives into languages and frameworks I'm using.
- **Projects** — what I'm building and what I've learned along the way.
- **Career & learning** — reflections on growing as a developer.
- **Things I find interesting** — technology, tools, and the occasional rabbit hole.

## Why Jekyll?

I chose [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com) for a few simple reasons:

1. **Speed** — static sites are fast.
2. **Simplicity** — Markdown + Liquid templates are easy to reason about.
3. **GitHub Pages** — free hosting, zero config deploys.

```ruby
# It's really this simple
jekyll new my-site
cd my-site
bundle exec jekyll serve
```
